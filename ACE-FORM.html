Rules

Welcome to PUP-ACE Form. Before proceeding, please be aware of the following rules. Make sure you understand well the instruction:

  • You can not process ACE if you are not officially enrolled
  • You can not add subject if you have no available units
  • You can not join a section if the total enrolled students in the said section is more than 75
  • You can not leave a section if the total enrolled students in the said section is less than or equal to 35
  • You can not join a section with conflict to your current schedule
  • You can not join a section with no available slot
  • You can not enroll a subject with prerequisite if the prerequiste is not yet passed
  • You can not re-enroll a subject if the immediate previous grade of the said subject is INC or No Grade
  • You can not enroll advance subjects
  • You ca not enroll subjects not in your curriculum
  • You can not enroll subjects with units more than your available units
  • You can not join a section if the total enrolled students in the said section is more than 75

By using this form, you agree to abide by the rules stated above.
